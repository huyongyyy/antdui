Attributes #

参数说明类型可选值默认
v-model内容String-
mode默认/简洁模式String"default", "simple"simple
placeholder空内容提示语String请输入内容...
excludeKeys排除掉某些工具栏菜单Array["headerSelect","italic","group-video","group-more-style","fullScreen",]
readOnly只读Booleanfalse
autoFocus聚焦Booleantrue
scroll是否支持滚动Booleantrue
editorStyle自定义样式String-
imgData上传图片自定义字段Objectfalse
imgFile上传图片接口用于接收文件的字段名称String"file"
imgAction上传图片方法Function-
imageView上传图片访问地址String-
imgMaxSize上传图片最大限制，单位（M）Number5
imgCompress上传图片是否压缩图片 只会对jpg做质量压缩Booleantrue
